Creating meaningful, easy-to-read titles is of great importance to our team. So, we have created 
this short guide to outline our naming convention preferences. All of these conventions are 
followed in our code, whatever the purpose of the file may be.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
VARIABLES
	- camelCase (Example: blockSucker, avidCollectorOfCeramicFigurines, potatoPeel)

FUNCTIONS (ROBOTC)
	- camelCase (Example: blockSucker, avidCollectorOfCeramicFigurines, potatoPeel)

FUNCTIONS (JAVA)
	- camelCase (Example: blockSucker, avidCollectorOfCeramicFigurines, potatoPeel)

CLASSES (JAVA)
	- PascalCase (Example: CarrotCake, MenuTemplate, SuckerPlate)

HEADERS (ROBOTC)
	- PascalCase (Example: CarrotCake, MenuTemplate, SuckerPlate)
